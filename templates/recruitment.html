Are you looking to improve your recruitment and hiring process? Have you identified suitable candidates for a job but want to ensure that you are making the right decision? Psychometric tests are the way to go.

On a monthly basis, we assist over 35 employers to apply our internationally recognised psychometric tests in Kenya with great results.

Why Do You Need The Psychometric Test?

  • They are objective and impersonal, allowing contenders to be compared in expertise terms without unconscious bias.
  • They help to make the recruiting process more efficient and can represent substantial HR costs.
  • You will only hire applicants suitable for the position.
  • They are proven to be reliable indicators of future work performance.

Types of Psychometric Assessments
